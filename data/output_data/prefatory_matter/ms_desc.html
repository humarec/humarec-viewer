MANUSCRIPT_CONTENT_SUMMARY:
Marciana Gr. Z 11 (379) contains the Acts, the Catholic letters and the letters of Paul in Greek, Latin and Arabic. This is the only trilingual Greek, Latin and Arabic manuscriptin the GA list. The manuscript is not dated but was estimated to the 12th or 13th century. It comes probably from Sicily.
